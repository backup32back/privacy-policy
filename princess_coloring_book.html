Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 11, 2025

Princess Coloring Book is a simple offline coloring game designed for children. We respect your privacy and do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information We Do Not Collect

The app:

  • does not collect personal data
  • does not track users
  • does not request account information
  • does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • does not collect location data
  • does not gather device identifiers

Children’s Privacy

This app is made for children and follows a strict “no data collected” approach. We do not collect any information from children or users of any age.

Offline Use

The game works completely offline. No internet connection is required for the app to function.

Advertising

The app contains no ads and does not include third-party advertising SDKs.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use analytics tools, third-party libraries, or external APIs that collect data.

Security

Because the app does not collect personal information, there is no personal data stored or transmitted.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
